Civic Lab Brussels was a meetup for people interested in creating open societal solutions through science and technology — what is often called public-interest tech and civic tech. At its biweekly open gatherings, all backgrounds welcome, you could present your civic project, join someone else's, or simply meet other engaged citizens in Brussels.
